History¶

FUndamental Library Language for Reverse Monte Carlo or fullrmc project has started in 2015 as a consequence of the incapability of modelling complex atomic systems’ structure. fullrmc is not a standard RMC software but it’s rather a molecular/atomic stochastic fitting platform to reverse modeling experimental data. fullrmc implementation exceeds in its capabilities and functionalities traditional RMC and Metropolis–Hastings algorithm. Therefore RMC appellation in fullrmc is not accurate but it’s retained to respecting the community terminology. Since fullrmc was published a first official release 0.2.0 was made. Later, successive releases were made as needed.
